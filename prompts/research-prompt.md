Research best practices for creating an accessible, high-converting personal website for a highly skilled professional offering consulting, training, and expert services.

## Key Research Areas

### 1. Accessibility (WCAG 2.1/2.2 Level AA)

- Color contrast requirements (4.5:1 minimum)
- Keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility
- Semantic HTML and ARIA labels
- Mobile accessibility standards
- Legal requirements (ADA, European Accessibility Act)

### 2. Design & UX Best Practices

- Professional layout principles (white space, visual hierarchy, grid systems)
- Typography best practices (hierarchy, readability, font pairing)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Navigation structure and user flow
- Current design trends (2024-2025) for professional portfolios

### 3. Essential Content & Structure

- Required sections: About, Services, Portfolio/Case Studies, Testimonials, Contact
- Content strategy for positioning expertise
- Portfolio presentation formats (case studies with results)
- Professional photography and visual branding
- Downloadable CV/resume best practices

### 4. SEO Optimization

- On-page SEO: meta tags, headers, schema markup, keywords
- Technical SEO: page speed (<3 sec), Core Web Vitals, mobile-first indexing
- Content SEO: thought leadership, backlink strategies
- Local SEO considerations for service providers

### 5. Conversion Optimization (CRO)

- CTA design and placement strategies
- Lead capture mechanisms (forms, booking systems, downloads)
- Trust elements (testimonials, credentials, social proof)
- Form optimization (minimal fields, clear value proposition)

### 6. Technical Requirements

- Performance optimization (image compression, caching, CDN)
- Hosting requirements (uptime, SSL, email integration)
- Analytics setup (GA4, heatmaps, conversion tracking)
- Platform comparison (WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, custom code)

### 7. Legal & Compliance

- Privacy policy, cookie consent, terms of service
- GDPR/CCPA compliance
- Professional licensing and disclaimers

### 8. Color Psychology & Branding

- Industry-appropriate color schemes for professional services
- Color psychology for trust, expertise, and credibility
- Accessibility considerations in color selection
- Recommended color palettes for different expert fields
- How to align colors with personal brand identity

## Deliverables

1. **Short implementation guideline** a short bullet point guideline that can be augmented with the expert's CV and preferred colour scheme
